Sometime in 2010, some coworkers were discussing the bizarre names nail polish shades have when someone said "that one sounds like a Star Trek episode." I vowed to code a Mental Floss-style this-or-that quiz, then it fell by the wayside. Until now.

Star Trek episode or nail polish colour?

All nail polish colours are real and were taken from the OPI and SpaRitual websites. All Star Trek episodes are from the original series. All coding by yours truly.

This is not a real quiz and there are no scores. It will keep looping through the names at random, in no particular order, and names will repeat. I may adjust the functionality at some point, but it's Friday night and I did this because the mood struck.
